📈 Task 1 题目示例

题目 Prompt

The bar chart below shows the percentage of students who passed their driving test after the first attempt in five different countries in 2020. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

📋 Task 1 写作模板

1
引言 (Introduction): 改写题目，不要照抄原文。"The bar chart illustrates the proportion of students who successfully passed their driving test on their first attempt across five countries in 2020."
2
概述 (Overview): 写2-3个最重要的趋势，不带具体数字。"Overall, it is clear that... while... Additionally, the most notable feature is..."
3
细节段落一 (Detail 1): 描述最高/最低的数据，带具体数字比较。使用 "at X%, considerably higher/lower than..."
4
细节段落二 (Detail 2): 描述其他数据，做对比。使用 "By contrast / In comparison / Similarly..."
💡 高分技巧： 使用多样化的词汇描述数据：rose sharply / declined gradually / remained relatively stable / accounted for / constituted / approximately / roughly / marginally

📖 练习文章

The Impact of Urbanization on Biodiversity

Urbanization is one of the most significant drivers of environmental change in the modern era. As cities expand rapidly across the globe, natural habitats are being fragmented and destroyed at an unprecedented rate. Scientists estimate that by 2050, approximately 70 percent of the world's population will reside in urban areas, placing immense pressure on ecosystems at the urban fringe.

Contrary to the popular belief that cities are ecological wastelands, recent research has demonstrated that urban environments can support surprisingly high levels of biodiversity. A study conducted in 2019 found that cities in temperate regions harbored on average 400 species of birds — a figure comparable to many rural landscapes. However, the species composition in cities tends to differ markedly from that in natural habitats, with generalist species replacing specialist ones.

The phenomenon of urban heat islands — where city centers are significantly warmer than surrounding rural areas — has been shown to affect the timing of biological events, a process known as phenology. Birds in city centers, for example, have been observed to begin their breeding season up to two weeks earlier than their counterparts in rural areas. This temporal shift can create mismatches between species and their food sources, potentially reducing reproductive success.

Green infrastructure, including parks, gardens, street trees, and green roofs, plays a crucial role in mitigating the negative effects of urbanization on wildlife. Research published in the journal Urban Ecosystems in 2021 revealed that cities with a higher proportion of green space supported greater species richness than those dominated by impervious surfaces. Furthermore, the connectivity between green patches — often described as ecological corridors — was found to be more important than the total area of green space alone.

Despite these findings, urban biodiversity remains an understudied field. Many existing conservation policies fail to account for the ecological value of urban areas, focusing predominantly on rural and wilderness settings. Experts argue that integrating biodiversity considerations into urban planning is not merely an environmental imperative but also a public health priority, given the well-documented benefits of nature exposure for human well-being.

🎧 听力技巧
核心策略 · 常见陷阱类型 · 备考建议

🌟 核心听力策略

👁

提前阅读题目 (Pre-reading)

在录音播放前，利用时间快速阅读所有题目，预判将要听到的内容类型。关注题目中的关键词：人名、地名、数字、时间等。

✏️

边听边记 (Note-taking)

使用缩写和符号快速记录关键信息。不要试图记录每一个词——专注于答案相关的关键信息：w= with, → = leads to, ↑ = increase。

🔄

注意同义替换 (Paraphrasing)

题目中的词通常不会在录音中原词出现，而是用同义词替换。例如：题目说 "expensive" → 录音说 "costs a lot / overpriced / not cheap"。

🔁

保持专注连贯 (Stay Focused)

如果错过了一题，立即放弃该题，集中注意力听下一题。不要因为一题而分心，以免错过更多答案。

📝

检查拼写和语法 (Check Answers)

录音结束后利用检查时间：核对拼写（尤其是专有名词）、检查填空格数限制是否超出、核实答案是否符合逻辑。

⚠️ 常见陷阱类型

陷阱 1：改变主意 (Change of Mind)

说话者给出一个答案后，又改变了主意。

"I'll book the red one... actually, no, let me go with the blue one."
▸ 正确答案是 blue，不是 red。

陷阱 2：否定词 (Negation)

在答案前加上否定词，容易听漏。

"The meeting is NOT on Monday, it's on Tuesday."
▸ 答案是 Tuesday。注意 not、never、hardly、rarely 等。

陷阱 3：数字混淆 (Numbers)

相似的数字容易听混，尤其是 -teen 和 -ty。

"thirteen (13)" vs "thirty (30)"，"fifteen (15)" vs "fifty (50)"
▸ 注意重音位置：thirTEEN vs THIRty

陷阱 4：干扰信息 (Distractors)

录音中会提到题目相关词汇，但不是正确答案。

题目问"停车费多少？" 录音说："The parking is usually $5, but today it's free."
▸ 答案是 free（免费），而非 $5。

陷阱 5：拼写错误 (Spelling)

人名和地名通常会拼读出来，必须正确拼写。

"My name is Catherine — that's C-A-T-H-E-R-I-N-E"
▸ 仔细听拼读，注意与常见拼写的区别（如 Katherine vs Catherine）

陷阱 6：条件限定 (Conditions)

答案可能带有时间、条件限定词。

"The library closes at 6pm on weekdays, but at 4pm on Saturdays."
▸ 若题目问周六关闭时间，答案是 4pm，不是 6pm。
